# Research Cover Letter Prompt

You are helping a Data Scientist Intern apply for a scientific research position. Your goal is to write a compelling, professional cover letter that bridges the gap between their current internship/project experience and the rigorous demands of a scientific research environment.

## Context
- **Candidate Background:** [Insert Candidate CV Highlights]
- **Target Role:** [Insert Job Description]
- **Organization Mission:** [Insert Research/Company Research Findings]

## Instructions
1. **Focus on Scientific Impact:** Connect the user's technical proficiency (e.g., PyTorch, NLP, Transformers) directly to the scientific discovery goals mentioned in the JD.
2. **Evidence-Based Tone:** Maintain a tone that is academic, rigorous, and results-oriented. Use phrases like "experimental design," "reproducible results," and "model interpretability."
3. **Bridge the Internship Gap:** Explicitly explain how their industry internship experience provides the operational excellence needed to accelerate scientific model development.
4. **Structure:**
   - **Introduction:** State the role and a high-level "thesis" for why the candidate is a fit.
   - **Body Paragraph 1 (Technical Depth):** Focus on a specific complex project and its relevance to the job.
   - **Body Paragraph 2 (Research Mission Alignment):** Show deep understanding of the lab's specific research area or company mission.
   - **Conclusion:** Reiterate enthusiasm and call to action.

## Key Themes to Emphasize
- Model development for scientific discovery.
- Bridging large-scale data engineering with nuanced scientific research.
- Commitment to rigorous evaluation and peer-reviewed standards.
